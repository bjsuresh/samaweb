ABOUT US

Supra Controls Pvt Ltd is an industry leader in data analytics and business intelligence specifically for Process Information Management Systems (PIMS) and Alarm Information Management System (AIMS) for the past 25 years. We have been assisting our customers in oil & gas refineries, pipelines, chemicals, petrochemicals, pharma, fertilizers, power generation & transmission, metal & mining, cement and smart cities with software solutions that cater to their plant operations, maintenance, safety, security and digitisation needs.
All our products work seamlessly with various control systems such as ABB, Emerson, Rockwell, Honeywell, Siemens, Hitachi, Schneider Electric, Triconex, Yokogawa as well as others through standard interfaces such as OPC or ODBC or TCP-IP or other data capture methods. Projects have been executed through automation vendors and directly with end customers that have been overseen by EIL, Technip, Toyo, L&T, TATA, Thyssenkrupp etc.
